PC and PE

WonderNetwork supports Minecraft on PC, iOS, Windows Phone and Android devices.
PC: Download at http://minecraft.net
Android: Search Minecraft: Pocket Edition on Google Play Store
iOS: Search Minecraft: Pocket Edition on App Store
Windows Phone: Search Minecraft: Pocket Editon on Windows App Store

We develop minigames ourselves and install them on our server.

On our Minecraft PC server network, we offer gamemodes such as:

Vanilla... Survive with your friends on no lag at all.
Pixelmon... Build your own pokemons to be the very best!


On our Minecraft PE server network, we offer minigames such as:

Spleef... Break snow under you, and knock people off to lava!
Party Games... Enjoy our minigames in random order.
Hunger Games... Fight until you are the last one left!
Survival... Survive with your friends on no lag at all!
Creative... Build as much as you want... with creative mode!
Factions... Create factions and take over one!
TNTRun... Run until you are the only one left! Remember: The ground is falling beneath you!
Zombie Apocalypse... Kill all zombies in the haunted house within 5 minutes!
Rescue the Villagers... The villagers has been lost throughout the world! Use spectator
mode to try and bring them back to their home!

How to Join

Our server currently supports MC:PE on later than the 0.11.0 update, and Minecraft PC on update 1.8.3.
PE:
Press the external tab, 
In server name type “WonderNetwork”
In IP/Address type “play.wonderlandpe.hol.es”
Press create
Tap on the server and play!
